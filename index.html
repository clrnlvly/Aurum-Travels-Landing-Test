Travel Tips and Hacks



TIPS

1. Plan your first night down to the last detail.
2. Photocopy your driver’s license, passport, visa, vaccination status (if required), and other important documents.
3. Buy all your adapters, SIM cards, and currency at home.
4. Alert your credit cards and financial institutions that you will be traveling and let them know your destination.
5. Pack a travel journal.
HACKS

1. Email a scanned copy of your passport to yourself so you have a backup in case it gets lost or stolen during your travels.
2. Roll your clothes for more space.
3. Sign up for last-minute deals on Headout.
4. If you're feeling jetlagged, try going for a run to help reset your body clock and energize yourself for the day ahead.
5. Make friends on the road using Travel Buddies and Backpack.
